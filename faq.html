We're glad you're interested! If you want to start a new chapter in the United States or India, please click here to learn more details!

This organization is designed to help students in India gain access to practical STEM education by creating and sustaining school-wide robotics programs. Chapters in the United States will be assigned to a school in India, and work to raise about $1,200. This will be used to purchase all the necessary items for a starter robotics kit, and send it to their paried school in India. Furthermore, they will work with and mentor their paired robotics team!

We have experience! Many of us have worked closely with FIRST teams all around the globe. In fact, our team members have even started programs in other conturies! Teaching has made a profound impact on our lives! Check out our testimonials!

For US Chapters: Any commited high school with members who will dedicate time to this project can become a chapter! Click here to get started! For Indian Chapters: Any secondary school can participate! Simple fill out this form to get started!
